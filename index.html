FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Plain-English answers to the things people ask most about foundli and finding a financial adviser.

How does foundli work?
foundli asks you 8 plain-English questions about your financial situation, goals and preferences. Our AI matches you with FCA-verified advisers who genuinely fit your needs — not just the nearest one. The process takes under 5 minutes and is completely free.
Is foundli free to use?
Yes. foundli is completely free for consumers, always. We never charge you to find or be matched with an adviser. foundli earns a referral fee from advisers when a successful match is made — this never affects which advisers we show you or how they are ranked.
Are the advisers on foundli FCA-regulated?
Yes. Every financial adviser shown on foundli is verified against the FCA (Financial Conduct Authority) register before they appear on our platform. We check this before any adviser goes live and re-verify regularly.

Check any adviser on the FCA register →
Will I receive cold calls after using foundli?
No. Only your matched adviser will contact you. foundli never sells your personal data or shares your details with multiple firms. You are in control of who gets in touch.
Does foundli give financial advice?
No. foundli matches consumers with FCA-regulated financial advisers. foundli does not provide financial advice and is not FCA-regulated. All advice is provided by the matched adviser, not by foundli. For free impartial guidance, visit MoneyHelper.
What types of adviser can foundli match me with?
foundli matches you with advisers specialising in pensions, retirement planning, investments, mortgages, inheritance, income protection, tax planning and general financial planning — matched to your specific situation.
How is foundli different from Unbiased or VouchedFor?
Unbiased and VouchedFor are directories — you search and scroll. foundli is a matching service — we ask the right questions first and introduce you to the adviser who genuinely fits. We also explain exactly why each adviser is a match for your situation, and we never accept payment to rank one adviser above another.
